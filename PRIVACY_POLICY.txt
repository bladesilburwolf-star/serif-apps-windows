SERIF OS // APPLICATION SUITE - PRIVACY POLICY

1. OVERVIEW
This Privacy Policy applies to the SERIF OS application suite and its 
associated standalone modules (including, but not limited to, Hub, 
VoiceSF, MixerSF, DocSF, EditorSF, ArcadeSF, DeskSF, MovieSF, ObjSF, 
and RecordSF). 

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This application 
is designed as a local-first software suite.

2. DATA COLLECTION AND STORAGE
- Local Execution: SERIF OS operates entirely on your local device. 
- Personal Information: We do not collect, store, transmit, or process 
  any personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive personal data.
- User Files: Any files, audio streams, recordings, or documents processed 
  or generated by the software remain strictly on your local machine and 
  are never uploaded to external servers.

3. NETWORK ACCESS & TELEMETRY
- SERIF OS does not track user activity, collect analytics, or transmit 
  telemetry data.
- The software does not connect to external third-party tracking services or 
  advertising networks.

4. PERMISSIONS
The application suite requests standard device permissions necessary only 
for local operations, such as:
- Local file system access (to open, edit, and save user files).
- Audio hardware access (for audio recording, voice changing, and playback).
- Display access (for screen capture/recording tools).

None of the data accessed through these permissions is transmitted off your 
device.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
SERIF OS does not integrate with third-party tracking APIs or data brokers.

6. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our 
software or legal requirements. The updated date at the top of this file 
will reflect the latest revision.

7. CONTACT INFORMATION
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please 
reach out via our official distribution channel or project support page.
